Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Exposure IQ is built with a "Privacy-First" philosophy. Our policy is simple:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, location data, or camera metadata.
  • On-Device Processing: All exposure calculations are performed locally on your device. No data ever leaves your phone.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use any analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer through the GitHub profile.
